Fedora Mobile.

Your one stop shop for interacting with Fedora's apps on the go.

The Community at Your Fingertips.

Fedora Mobile provides access to the Fedora Community wherever you are. Whether you're at a FOSS event and want to earn some Fedora Badges, or you're trying to make your way to a FUDCon and need a map right away, Fedora Mobile has you covered.

"It's really cool and you should use it."

— Nobody Ever

Did we break something?

Status is Fedora Mobile's interface to the Fedora Infrastructure Status page. It allows you to see which, if any, Fedora services are down and offers a brief explaination as to why.

TL;DR

We love breaking things and it's probably our fault.

Search Packages.

Ever wonder if something is packaged for Fedora but not have access to a computer at that moment? Probably not. But maybe you will some day, and now you can just pull out your phone or tablet and check using Fedora Mobile's interface to the Fedora Packages API which provides a wealth of information about every Fedora package.

TL;DR

We ♥ run-on sentences.

Obtain & Manage Fedora Badges.

If you're at a FOSS event, you can scan QR codes and obtain new Fedora Badges to show off. You can also check which badges you and other people have already obtained and view information about them.

TL;DR

Badges make you a really cool person and you should earn lots of them to show everyone how cool you are.

Know About Upcoming Events.

Stay in the know about upcoming Fedora events such as FUDCon, Flock, and Fedora Activity days. Fedora Mobile makes it easy to subscribe to Fedora's Fedocal calendar by integrating it with Android's own Calendar app.

TL;DR

I don't actually have a TL;DR for this section. Events are serious business.

Fedora Account System.

Fedora Mobile lets you synchronize your Fedora Account System account with the Android system. This makes it easy for you to decide what information gets synchronized and what information gets left alone.

TL;DR

You only have to sign in once to use our awesome app effectively, then your phone will store your authentication info thereafter.

Fedora Message Bus.

Instantly know when something happens that you care about. Fedora Mobile integrates with the Fedora Message Bus (fedmsg) to provide you instant notifications about topics that you subscribe to. We use the Google Cloud Messaging system, which saves battery life on your phone or tablet, since we don't need to hold a constant connection to the fedmsg stream.

TL;DR

We know you get too much sleep, so we'll do everything in our power to send you notifications at the worst times, just to wake you up.
